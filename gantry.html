Test Bed Gantry

Attached above is the CAD assembly I created for a gantry for the float table test Bed at my research lab. There is an acrylic cover on the front I manufactured so if there are any air compression explosions lab members will be safe. The gantry also holds a camera at the top which is used to determine the position of the float bots on the table. This test table is used to simulate microgravity conditions for space robotics projects including REAACH.

The CAD assembly was used for a few purposes. One it was used to determine the cost and which parts are necessary for the gantry. It was also used to determine the dimensions of specific parts like the acrylic cover which made assembly easier.

I was a part of the assembly team putting together this gantry including cutting the 80/20, tapping the insides of the 80/20 and putting it together. In the future there is a plan to add stepper motors to the gantry to add more degrees of freedom to testing on this table.
